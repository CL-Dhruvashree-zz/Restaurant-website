Coffee is brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain flowering plants in the Coffee genus. From the coffee fruit, the seeds are separated to produce a stable, raw product: unroasted green coffee. The seeds are then roasred, a process which transforms them into a consumable product: roasted coffee, which is ground into fine particles that are typically steeped in hot water before being filtered out , producing a cuo of coffee.
